Call for Papers

Paper submissions are invited in the areas of next-generation computing and communication systems towards intelligent technologies. Papers submitted to NexTCom-2026 must be original and must not have been previously published or accepted for publication elsewhere. Additionally, submissions must not be under consideration for any other event or publication during the entire review process. All papers must be submitted electronically by the submission deadline through a CMT link. Submitted papers will undergo a peer review process coordinated by the Technical Program Committee (TPC).
Submit here: CMT Submission Portal
Note:- The Microsoft CMT service was used for managing the peer-reviewing process for this conference. This service was provided for free by Microsoft and they bore all expenses, including costs for Azure cloud services as well as for software development and support.

Submission Guidelines

All papers must include title, abstract, keywords, and be in PDF format. Follow the manuscript template before submission.
